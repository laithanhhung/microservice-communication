---
title: "Asynchronous / Non-blocking"
---

**Overview**:
The caller does **not** wait for the callee to complete the operation.

**Key Patterns**:
- Message Queues (Point-to-Point)
- Publish/Subscribe (Topics)
- Polling / Callbacks

**Use Cases**:
- Background processing (email sending, report generation).
- High-throughput systems.
